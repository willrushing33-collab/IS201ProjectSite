What is Pokémon LeafGreen?

Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen (2004) are enhanced remakes of the original Pokémon Red and Green games, developed by Game Freak for the Game Boy Advance. LeafGreen puts you in the shoes of a young trainer setting off from Pallet Town on a journey to catch 'em all, defeat eight Gym Leaders, take down Team Rocket, and become the Pokémon Champion.

Released in the United States on September 9, 2004, these games introduced an entirely new generation of players — including me — to the Kanto region. With updated graphics, refreshed mechanics, and the beloved Sevii Islands post-game, LeafGreen is widely considered one of the greatest handheld RPGs ever made.

The iconic box art featuring Venusaur, the Grass-type starter's final evolution.

Choose Your Starter

At the beginning of your journey, Professor Oak offers you one of three Pokémon. Here's a breakdown of each, ranked by my personal experience:

  1. 🔥 Charmander (Fire-type) — The hardest early game, but the most rewarding.
    • Evolves into Charmeleon at Lv. 16, then the legendary Charizard at Lv. 36
    • Struggles against the first two Gym Leaders (Brock & Misty)
    • Best for experienced players who want a challenge
  2. 💧 Squirtle (Water-type) — The balanced, smooth playthrough choice.
    • Evolves into Wartortle and then Blastoise
    • Great defense and versatile moveset throughout the game
    • Easily handles early Gyms with Water-type advantage
  3. 🌿 Bulbasaur (Grass/Poison-type) — The easiest early game starter.
    • Evolves into Ivysaur and finally Venusaur (the box art Pokémon!)
    • Dominates the first two Gyms with type advantages
    • Great choice for first-time players or speedrunners

Essential Tips for New Players

  • 🎒 Always keep Antidotes and Potions in your bag — Poison damage outside of battle can knock out your party
  • 🐟 Catch an Abra as early as possible — it evolves into the extremely powerful Kadabra/Alakazam
  • 🗺️ Don't skip the Sevii Islands post-game — they contain rare Pokémon unavailable in Kanto
  • 💾 Save before every important battle or encounter with a legendary Pokémon
  • 🔄 Trade with FireRed to complete the full Pokédex — some Pokémon are version-exclusive
  • ⚡ Teach your Pokémon HMs like Cut, Surf, and Fly as soon as possible to access new areas
  • 🏆 Level balance your team — relying on one powerhouse Pokémon will hurt you at the Elite Four
